Design System Inspiration of Slite

An inspired interpretation of Slite's design language — a warm-cream knowledge-base system that pairs pastel section bands (peach, blush, sage, butter) with a dark-navy ink and a single deep-blue CTA, all anchored on a generous 50-pixel pill-button radius and Garnett's stylistic-set display type.

Section 6 · Elevation

Touch Targets

  • Buttons hold ~44–48 px tap height (8–12 px vertical padding + 1.5 line-height + 13.6-px text). Meets WCAG AAA 44 × 44.
  • Nav links collapse into a stacked drawer at < 845 px; each row at least 48 px tall.
  • Form inputs sit at 44 px tall, comfortable for thumb input.
  • Pill tags (~28 px tall) are read-only labels — reduced size acceptable.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Nav: utility links collapse behind a hamburger at < 845 px; brand wordmark + dark-ink "Start for free" pill stay visible at every breakpoint.
  • Pricing grid: 3 → 2 → 1 across desktop → tablet → mobile. Featured tier always centered on desktop, top on mobile.
  • Pastel feature grid (4-up): collapses 4 → 3 → 2 → 1.
  • Customer-story row: avatar pair stacks above quote at mobile.
  • Display type: 64 → 48 → 36 → 32 px down the breakpoint ladder; line-height stays proportional.
  • Pastel section bands always full-bleed; cream canvas between bands is what shrinks.
